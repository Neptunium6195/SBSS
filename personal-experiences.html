Personal Experiences

Here are some of our personal experiences with volunteering:

Testemonial #1

I currently volunteer at a non-profit located in Blessings for Your Soles as a Website Developer. This volunteer opportunity has helped me gain experience is website development, collaboration, and a professional work field.

Testemonial #2

Once in a while I like to volunteer at food banks with my church. I was able to help those that suffered from the Pallisades fire as well as local people at parks in need for food. Volunteering at food pantries are quite underrated, your help of a couple of hours in the morning allows for more meals and people to be served. Volunteering at food banks are also a great way to build relationships with fellow volunteers and people in your community.
